"What whole thing?"

"Why, the whole thing. There ain't but one; how we set the runaway nigger free—me and Tom."

"Good land! Set the run—What is the child talking about! Dear, dear, out of his head again!"

"No, I ain't out of my head; I know all what I'm talking about. We did set him free—me and Tom. We laid out to do it, and we done it. And we done it elegant, too." He'd got a start, and she never checked him up, just set and stared and stared, and let him clip along, and I see it warn't no use for me to put in.
