They made the king tell his yarn, and they made the old gentleman tell his'n; and anybody but a lot of prejudiced chuckleheads would a seen that the old gentleman was spinning truth and t'other one lies. And by and by they had me up to tell what I knowed.

The king he give me a left-handed look out of the corner of his eye, and so I knowed enough to talk on the right side.
